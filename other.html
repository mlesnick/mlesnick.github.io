A Game

Here are the rules to "Nails", a card game I designed in 2010.  It's a very simple SET-like game with a dynamic similar to the children's card game War.  The game is played with a custom deck of 48 round cards. The goal is to decide quickly whether a pair of cards is distance two apart in a certain metric; for a randomly chosen pair of cards, the probability of this is quite close to 1/2.   You can download a PDF file of the cards here, print them on cardstock (8 color pages), and cut them out.
