Hotels Etc.

If you are looking for a hotel near the venue, there are a number of hotels near the Main St. 7 station in Flushing. The hotels in this area will be less ~1.8 miles from the venue; a short cab ride / bus ride away, or a ~35 minute walk.

Otherwise, there are zillions of places to stay throughout the city, and though Flushing has some great food, you might prefer to stay somewhere more centrally located, if you plan to explore NYC while you are in town. It is easy to get between the west side of Manhattan and Flushing via LIRR @ Penn Station. Airbnb may be a good option. Please let us know if you have any questions about accommodations or want some suggestions about neighborhoods, etc.
