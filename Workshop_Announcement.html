Workshop on Topological Data Analysis

Kyoto University, June 6-7 2014, 9:30 a.m. - 5:30 p.m

Room 108, Department of Mathematics

Organizers:
Yasu Hiraoka (Kyushu), Michael Lesnick (IMA)

About:
With many researchers in topological data analysis traveling to Kyoto for SoCG 2014, we decided to organize a small, informal workshop just before the main event to give a few of us a chance to share our recent research. Talks will focus on topological data analysis and persistence theory.
