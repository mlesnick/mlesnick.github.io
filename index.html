Research Interests

I'm a mathematician working primarily on topological data analysis (TDA).   My research focuses on the theoretical foundations of TDA, and also on the computational and applied aspects of the subject.   A lot of my work is on multiparameter persistence; see this introduction and also these course notes.

Software

RIVET is practical tool for the visualization and analysis of two-parameter persistent homology, designed by Matthew Wright and me, and developed by several people.
